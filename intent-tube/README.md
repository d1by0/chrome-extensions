# IntentTube 🧘📺

> **Turn passive watching into purposeful viewing.**
>
> *Distraction-free YouTube. Built for focus.*

---

## 💡 Why IntentTube?

I’ve always been someone who binges YouTube. Whenever something is on my mind, I tend to turn to creators I admire or look up to. But lately, the experience has been frustrating—especially when the first thing I see on a video is an ad.

Initially, I thought about building another adblocker or a simple "unhook" extension. But eventually, it made me realize that the problem runs much deeper than ads. It is the entire distraction engine—the infinite recommendation feeds, clickbait thumbnails, autoplay loops, and endless comments—designed specifically to capture and monetize our attention.

**IntentTube** is my answer to this. It's not just about hiding ads; it's about introducing intentionality. It forces a mindful friction before entering the YouTube stream, transforming a passive binging habit into a focused, learning-oriented tool.

---

## ✨ Key Features

1. **🧘 The Intent Gate (Anti-Binge Barrier)**
   - When you open YouTube, the recommendation feed is completely hidden behind a beautiful, minimalistic overlay.
   - It asks you: *"What are you here to watch or learn?"*
   - Submitting your query searches YouTube directly, keeping you on track without getting sidetracked by recommended videos.

2. **🎬 Clean Theater Mode**
   - Automatically hides recommendation sidebars, comments section, and live chats.
   - Cleans up distractions so you can focus entirely on the video content.
   - Option to hide YouTube Shorts.

3. **📝 Timestamped Notes**
   - A built-in, collapsible notes sidebar.
   - Type notes as you watch; pressing `Enter` saves the note along with a clickable timestamp link (e.g., `[12:34] - Code example`).
   - Export your notes directly to a clean Markdown file.

4. **⏱️ Zen Timer**
   - Set a focus duration for your session (e.g., 20 or 30 minutes).
   - A subtle countdown indicator in the corner.
   - Once the timer expires, the video player is gently blurred and paused, prompting you to take a mindful break.
